Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

Profile

Full Name: Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam

Born: 15 October 1931

Profession: Scientist, President of India

Nationality: Indian

Biography

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a major role in India's missile development and nuclear program. He was known as the "Missile Man of India".

Achievements

  • Bharat Ratna Award
  • Padma Bhushan
  • Padma Vibhushan
  • 11th President of India (2002-2007)
